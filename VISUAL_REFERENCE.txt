## 🎬 ANIMATIONS & INTERACTIONS

Smooth Transitions:
- All interactive elements: 0.3s cubic-bezier easing
- Button hover: Lift up (translateY -2px) + shadow increase
- Cards: Smooth shadow expansion on hover
- Inputs: Border color change + glow on focus

Slide-In Animation:
- Notifications slide in from right (400px → 0px)
- Duration: 0.3s ease-out
- Remove with fade-out

Pulse Animation:
- Timer when under 60 seconds: opacity 1 → 0.5 → 1
- Violation warning countdown: same pulse effect
- Duration: 1s infinite
